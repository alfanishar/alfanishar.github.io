// ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Alfa Nishar NK, a creative individual with diverse interests and passions. I was born on June 19, 2007, and I go by several nicknames including Munna, Nisha, Allu, and SEO Young.

My creative journey includes a love for drawing, where I express my thoughts and emotions through art. Music is another significant part of my life - I enjoy listening to various genres and find inspiration in melodies and lyrics.

As a proud BTS fan, I appreciate their music and message of self-love and positivity. My favorite actor is SK, and I admire Hanasha for their talent and personality.

When I'm not creating art or enjoying music, you can find me chilling with friends, exploring new ideas, or simply enjoying the simple moments in life.

Feel free to connect with me on Instagram @alllf_hh where I share glimpses of my creative journey and daily life.
